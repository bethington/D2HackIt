PvpBuddy 1.0 for D2HackIt!
=====================================================================
This module helps you out in PvP games by hiding ears and gold to
reduce lag.

Commands:
  .pvpbuddy ears <level>  - Hides ears from character below <level>.
  .pvpbuddy gold <amount> - Hides gold below <amount>.
  .pvpbuddy settings      - Show current PvPBuddy settings.
  .pvpbuddy help          - Shows this help

Installation:
  Copy PvPBuddy.d2h and PvPBuddy.ini to your D2HackIt folder and use
  ".load pvpbuddy" to load the module.

Tips:
  ".pvpbuddy ears 100" and ".pvpbuddy gold 1000000" hides all ears 
  and all gold.
